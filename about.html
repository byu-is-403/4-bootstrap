About me

I am a student living in Provo, UT. After years of trying to find my course in life, I discovered the Information Systems program and decided to enroll in its prerequisite courses. The more I progressed through those courses, the more I knew applying technology to solve business problems is what I wanted to do.

In my free time, I like to make and listen to music. My first passion was playing the drums and it occupied the majority of my time in middle school and high school.

After my sophomore year of college, I spent 2 years in Spain. Since returning to the United States, I still enjoy finding time to practice my Spanish and take part in their unique culture. I have travelled to Paris and spent a summer living in Ottawa, Canada. I would love to continue traveling throughout my life.
